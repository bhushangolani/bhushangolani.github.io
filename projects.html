CSE 595 · Course Project

Child-Centered LLM Safety Evaluation & Alignment

An end-to-end LLM safety pipeline for children (ages 6–12). Built a human-verified dataset of child-style prompts based on the 4Cs framework. Benchmarked 10 open LLMs using GPT-5 as an LLM-as-a-judge, scoring responses on safety, age-appropriateness, empathy, and boundary-setting. Trained a safety classifier using XLM-RoBERTa and applied DPO for child-centered alignment.

EECS 595 · NLP

GPT-style Language Model & Conversational SFT

Implemented a GPT-style transformer in PyTorch from scratch with multi-head causal self-attention, Rotary Position Embeddings (RoPE), SwiGLU feed-forward layers, and RMSNorm. Built a scalable pretraining pipeline on FineWeb-Edu and designed a supervised fine-tuning (SFT) framework for conversational agents with selective loss masking.
